Welcome to FC 25 & FIFA 25!

With the release of FC 25, soccer gaming takes a leap forward. Expect enhanced graphics, improved gameplay mechanics, and the introduction of new dynamic features that allow for more strategic play. FIFA 25 adds new leagues and teams, bringing a more realistic experience to the field.

FC 25 introduces the much-anticipated Career Mode, allowing players to build their dream team from scratch, manage every aspect of their club, and compete for the ultimate glory. FIFA 25 enhances the experience with online multiplayer modes, including FUT (FIFA Ultimate Team), which offers even more customization options.
